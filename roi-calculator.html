How to Calculate ROI for Side Hustles

Return on Investment (ROI) helps you measure the efficiency of an investment or compare the efficiency of several different investments. For a side hustle, your "investment" might be cash (tools, software, inventory) and time. While this calculator focus on the financial payback period, always keep in mind the opportunity cost of your time. If it takes years to pay back a $500 investment while earning $2/hr, you might want to rethink your strategy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a good payback period?
For small side hustles, a payback period of 3-6 months is excellent. Anything under a year is generally considered a strong investment for a low-capital business.
Does ROI account for taxes?
Ideally, yes. You should use your *net profit* (after taxes) to calculate a more realistic ROI and payback period.
How do I calculate growth?
Monthly growth can be estimated based on your marketing efforts or past performance. Even a small 5% monthly growth can significantly shorten your payback period through compounding.
